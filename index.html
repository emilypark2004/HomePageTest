Hi, I'm Emily Park!

I'm a second-year student at UCSD, and I'm majoring in computer science. I'm highly passionate about computer science as it enables me to explore my interest in applying my problem-solving skills and creativity towards creating functional machines and programs. I have previously interned for The Boeing Company and the Brea Museum & Historical Society. I'm currently a Tech Fellow/Teaching Assistant for CodePath.org's iOS development courses, and I'm also serving as a project developer and mentor for CSforeach here at UCSD. I'm seeking to invest myself in internship and research opportunities to continue to build onto my skillset in preparation for industry roles.
